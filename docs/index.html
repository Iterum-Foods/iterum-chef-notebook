What We're Building Will Change Everything

A revolutionary platform that transforms how all food professionals work - from home cooks sharing recipes online to YouTube creators scaling content, from small restaurants to enterprise catering operations. The future of food management is coming.

Smart Recipe Organization

AI-powered recipe categorization and search. Automatically organize by cuisine, dietary restrictions, cooking method, or prep time. Find any recipe in your library instantly, whether you manage 50 or 5,000 recipes.

Professional Ingredient Library

291+ professional ingredients with cost tracking, seasonality, and allergen data. Perfect for restaurants managing inventory, caterers pricing events, or private chefs sourcing specialty items.

Recipe Scaling & Costing

Instantly scale recipes from 2 portions to 200. Automatic cost calculations for accurate pricing. Essential for catering companies bidding events and restaurants managing food costs.

Menu Planning & Client Management

Build custom menus from your recipe library. Track client preferences, dietary restrictions, and favorite dishes. Perfect for private chefs managing multiple clients and consultants developing restaurant concepts.

Business Analytics & Reporting

Track your most profitable recipes, popular dishes, and cost trends. Generate professional reports for restaurant owners, catering proposals, or consulting presentations. Data-driven decisions for every business.

Team Collaboration & Sharing

Share recipes securely with your team or clients. Set permissions for viewing vs. editing. Perfect for restaurant chains, catering teams, or consultants working with multiple venues.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the professional recipe management platform

How is this different from regular recipe apps?

+

Unlike consumer recipe apps, we're built specifically for professional kitchens. We handle recipe scaling from 2 to 2,000 portions, real-time cost calculations with 291+ professional ingredients, inventory management, and business analytics. Think of it as the difference between a calculator and accounting software.

Who is this platform for?

+

Everyone who works with food! From home cooks sharing recipes online to YouTube creators scaling content, from small restaurants to enterprise catering operations. We're building features for food bloggers, cookbook authors, culinary schools, meal prep services, and any professional or passionate cook who wants to revolutionize their recipe management.

When does beta testing begin?

+

Beta testing begins Q1 2025 with select waitlist members getting first access. We're currently in active development and will roll out testing phases starting with our most engaged community members. Join the waitlist to secure your spot in the first wave of beta testing.

How much will it cost?

+

Pricing starts at $99/month for restaurants and private chefs, $199/month for catering companies, and custom pricing for enterprise clients. Waitlist members get 50% off for the first year. We also offer a 30-day free trial to test everything in your kitchen.

Can I import my existing recipes?

+

Absolutely! Our AI can import recipes from photos, PDFs, Word docs, handwritten notes, existing software, or spreadsheets. We support bulk imports and will help migrate your entire recipe library during onboarding - no matter how it's currently stored.

What kind of support do you provide?

+

Every customer gets dedicated onboarding, recipe migration assistance, training for your team, and ongoing support. We also have a comprehensive help center, video tutorials, and live chat support during business hours. Enterprise customers get dedicated account management.
