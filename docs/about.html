Our Story

From kitchen chaos to culinary innovation - how we're solving the problems we experienced firsthand in professional kitchens.

The Problem We Lived

As culinary professionals, we've all experienced the same frustrations: recipes scattered across notebooks, spreadsheets, and sticky notes. Manual scaling that takes hours and still results in errors. No way to track costs accurately or manage inventory efficiently.

We've worked in restaurants where recipe management was pure chaos - where a simple scaling error could cost thousands in wasted ingredients or lost business opportunities. We've seen talented chefs spend more time on paperwork than cooking.

The Solution We Built

Instead of complaining about the problem, we decided to solve it. We combined our deep culinary experience with modern technology to create a platform that actually understands the needs of professional kitchens.

We're not outsiders building for the industry - we're industry insiders building the tools we always needed. Every feature, every workflow, every decision is informed by real kitchen experience.
