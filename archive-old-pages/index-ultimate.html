FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Iterum Foods

What platforms are included in Iterum Foods?
Iterum Foods includes four integrated platforms: Culinary R&D (recipe development and menu planning), Business Planner (financial projections and market analysis), Payroll System (time tracking and payroll processing), and Skills Portfolio (professional networking). You can subscribe to individual platforms or get all four with our Professional plan.
How does the waitlist work?
Simply join the waitlist by filling out the form. You'll receive a position number and be notified via email when it's your turn. We're onboarding users in batches to ensure the best experience. There's no obligation - joining the waitlist is completely free.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start your trial. You can cancel anytime during the trial period with no charges.
Can I switch between platforms?
Absolutely! If you start with one platform, you can upgrade to include additional platforms anytime. Your data is seamlessly integrated across all platforms in your account.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We use enterprise-grade security with encrypted data storage, regular backups, and comply with all data protection regulations. Your recipes, financial data, and employee information are completely secure and private.
Do you offer support?
Yes! All plans include email support. Professional and Enterprise plans get priority support with faster response times. Enterprise customers also receive a dedicated account manager and phone support.
