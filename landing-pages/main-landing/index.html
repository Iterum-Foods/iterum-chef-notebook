Questions from the Kitchen

Chef's Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your digital culinary companion

What makes Iterum Foods different from other restaurant software?
Iterum Foods is built BY chefs, FOR chefs. We understand the culinary workflow because we've lived it. Our notebook-style interface feels natural to culinary professionals, and we integrate recipe development, business planning, payroll, and networking - no other platform offers this complete ecosystem designed specifically for the culinary industry.
How does the waitlist work for chefs?
Simply join the waitlist by filling out the form. You'll receive a position number and be notified via email when it's your turn. We're onboarding culinary professionals in batches to ensure personalized support and the best possible experience. There's no obligation - joining is completely free.
Is there a free trial for chefs?
Absolutely! All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start. We want you to experience the full power of your digital culinary notebook before you commit. Cancel anytime during the trial with no charges.
Can I import my existing recipes and menus?
Yes! You can import recipes from PDFs, Word documents, images, or even by pasting URLs from recipe websites. Our AI-powered parser extracts ingredients, instructions, and yields automatically. You can also import menus from existing PDFs or images. Your culinary knowledge, digitized in minutes.
Is my recipe data secure and private?
Absolutely. We understand that your recipes are your intellectual property. All data is encrypted, regularly backed up, and stored securely in the cloud. Your recipes, financial data, and employee information are completely private and protected. We'll never share your culinary secrets.
Do you offer support for culinary professionals?
Yes! All plans include email support from our team (many of whom are former chefs). Professional and Enterprise plans get priority support with faster response times. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager and phone support. We speak your language - culinary and technical.
