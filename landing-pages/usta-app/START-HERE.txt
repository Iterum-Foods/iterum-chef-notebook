WHAT IS USTA?
-------------
Usta (Turkish: "master craftsman") is a professional skills platform
combining:
• TikTok's ease (trend-based skill challenges)
• LinkedIn's value (professional validation & jobs)
• Masterclass's depth (learn from experts)
• Duolingo's engagement (daily practice, XP, levels)
• Instagram's social (follow, like, share)

MARKET: 50M+ skilled professionals in US
REVENUE: $42M by Year 5
MODEL: Free for users, monetize via jobs/premium/ads
