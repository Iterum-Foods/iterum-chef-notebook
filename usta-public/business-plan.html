Section 2

The Problem

For Skilled Workers:

  • No way to showcase skills: LinkedIn is text-based. Instagram isn't professional. Portfolios are hard to build.
  • Creative pressure: What should I create? How do I film it? Is it good enough?
  • Can't prove abilities: Resumes show claims, not proof of actual skills.
  • Fragmented presence: Skills scattered across multiple platforms.
  • Weak professional network: LinkedIn is too generic, not industry-specific.
  • No practice system: Hard to stay sharp and improve consistently.

For Employers:

  • Can't assess real skills: Resumes show experience, not actual capability.
  • Expensive recruitment: $3-5K per hire, 45+ days to fill positions.
  • High turnover: 73% leave in first year due to skill mismatch.
  • Time-consuming: Review hundreds of resumes manually.
  • No verification: Can't trust claimed abilities without testing.
"$12B spent annually on recruitment, training, and professional networking‚Äî with no platform that works for skilled trades."
Section 3

The Usta Solution

Core Innovation: Trend-based skill challenges created by Master Ustas. No creative pressure‚Äîjust follow the guide, demonstrate your skill, get validated.

How It Works:

  1. Master Usta posts #WeldingBasics challenge with perfect technique
  2. Includes recording guide: "Shot 1: Setup, Shot 2: Weld, Shot 3: Result"
  3. Users tap "Try This Challenge"
  4. App guides them through exact same shots
  5. Users record their version
  6. Post & get validated by professionals
  7. Skill added to portfolio automatically
  8. Earn XP, level up, build credibility

Go-to-Market Strategy

Phase 1: Community Seeding (Months 1-3)

  • Launch with 5 craft communities: Welding, Culinary, Electrical, Automotive, Carpentry
  • Recruit 25 Master Ustas per craft (125 total)
  • Each Master Usta brings 50-100 followers
  • Target: 10K users by Month 3

Phase 2: Viral Growth (Months 4-9)

  • Challenge virality (TikTok mechanics)
  • Cross-craft discovery
  • Employer partnerships
  • Target: 75K users by Month 9

Phase 3: Scale (Months 10-18)

  • Expand to 20 craft categories
  • Launch job board monetization
  • Premium features rollout
  • Target: 250K users by Month 18
Growth Drivers: Viral challenges (50% of growth), Master Usta recruitment (30%), word-of-mouth (15%), paid acquisition (5%)
